Frequently asked questions

Can decentralized hosting improve website uptime?

Yes, decentralized hosting minimizes the risk of downtime by distributing your content across multiple nodes, making your site more resilient to server failures and network issues.

Which cryptocurrencies are supported by crypto payment gateways?

Most modern crypto payment gateways support popular cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, and others, with options to add support for additional tokens as needed.

How scalable are backend systems in blockchain applications?

With the right architecture, backend systems can scale seamlessly to handle high user loads and complex blockchain operations, ensuring smooth performance even under pressure.

Is decentralized hosting suitable for Web3 projects?

Absolutely. Decentralized hosting aligns perfectly with the principles of Web3, offering transparency, ownership, and freedom from centralized control for dApps and user content.

What role does the backend play in processing crypto payments?

The backend handles transaction logic, wallet integrations, confirmation tracking, and security protocols—making it the core engine that powers reliable crypto payment systems.
